Final experiment summary

1. Financial experiments are fully complete and form the main empirical result.
2. Replay128 was the strongest practical financial setting.
3. Default selective adaptation remained a strong sparse baseline.
4. On financial, top-k=4 approximately matched full LoRA mean accuracy with fewer updates.
5. ArXiv provided a real second-domain benchmark.
6. On arXiv, oracle-triggered adaptation improved over no-update, but detector-driven adaptation did not reliably activate.
7. Therefore, the paper's strongest claim is robust financial drift adaptation, with supporting cross-domain evidence on arXiv under oracle drift windows.
