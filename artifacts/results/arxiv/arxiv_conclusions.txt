ArXiv summary

1. The arXiv domain loader, temporal split, and adaptation pipeline executed successfully on a real public second-domain dataset.
2. Detector-driven adaptation did not reliably trigger on arXiv under the current detector calibration, so most non-oracle settings collapsed to the no-update regime.
3. Oracle-triggered adaptation improved over no-update:
   - Accuracy: 0.5491 vs 0.5303
   - Macro F1: 0.3522 vs 0.3150
   - Weighted F1: 0.5431 vs 0.5164
4. These results show cross-domain adaptability under annotated drift windows, while detector calibration for arXiv remains future work.
